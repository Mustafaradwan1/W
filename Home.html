What is home insurance?

Medical insurance, also known as health insurance, is a policy that covers the cost of private healthcare. Depending on your level of cover, it can be used to pay for everything from private hospital stays to diagnostic procedures, surgery, physiotherapy and more.

You can take out health insurance for yourself, for you and a partner, for your child, or even for your whole family.
